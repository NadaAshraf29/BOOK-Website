Our Story

Logic? Ah, yes, it is a concept that lives in other worlds while I, I am in love with a life full of imagination and madness. I love life passionately, but it is not just any life, it is an imaginary life, immersed in dreams that arose from books and grew in size until they broke the ceiling of reality. But there is nothing wrong with enjoying a little madness from time to time. I chose to make my madness embodied in the pages of the novels I read, and live the lives of heroes and heroines in their imaginary worlds. With every new page I open, I leave the real world and immerse myself in the worlds of imagination, surrounded by books Paper and digital files to continue my journey. It is an unforgettable journey, but before I begin, I must put my phone in airplane mode, to ensure that I do not disconnect from this imaginary world until I finish reading. Hence, I welcome you to my world, where imagination has no limits, and dreams turn into reality between the pages of books.

That's why I wanted to share my love and madness with you and create Air Plane Mood. Also, if you don't read and want to try, we will make you happy to find the right book for you and discuss with your friends in a group like WhatsApp. We are waiting for you to share the path with us and write together the first line of the novel
